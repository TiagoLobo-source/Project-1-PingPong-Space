Instructions

The objective of the game is to prevent the 2 balls from passing the limits of the canvas sides (left and right). If a ball passes beyond a paddle, the opposing player scores a point. The Player that reaches the 3rd point first wins.

Player 1 controls:

Move the paddle up: Press the "W" key.

Move the paddle down: Press the "S" key.

Player 2 controls:

Move the paddle up: Press the "up arrow" key.

Move the paddle down: Press the "down arrow" key.
